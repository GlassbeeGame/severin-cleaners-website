# Brand Voice Profile — Severin Hauling

> Read this before writing any copy for the website. Feed these rules into any content skill (seo-content, direct-response-copy, etc.).

---

## Who We Sound Like

A knowledgeable local tradesman explaining junk removal over the phone — direct, helpful, specific, no BS. Think: the guy who's hauled junk from 10,000 San Diego homes and knows every neighborhood by name.

## Who We Don't Sound Like

A content marketing agency. A corporate website. An AI chatbot. A generic "we're committed to excellence" service company.

---

## Voice Attributes

| Be This | Never Be This |
|---------|--------------|
| Direct and specific | Vague or fluffy |
| Knowledgeable local | Generic national brand |
| Helpful and practical | Salesy or pushy |
| Confident (we do this daily) | Arrogant or boastful |
| Conversational | Formal or corporate |
| Honest about pricing | Evasive about costs |

---

## Vocabulary Rules

### Banned Words and Phrases

Never use these — they signal AI-generated or agency content:

- "delve" / "delving into"
- "comprehensive" / "comprehensively"
- "leverage" / "leveraging"
- "streamline" / "streamlined"
- "navigate" / "navigating"
- "landscape" (as metaphor)
- "utilize" (say "use")
- "facilitate"
- "in today's world" / "in today's fast-paced world"
- "We are committed to..."
- "Our team of professionals..."
- "Exceptional service"
- "State-of-the-art"
- "Cutting-edge"
- "Hassle-free" (overused)
- "One-stop shop"
- "Your go-to source"

### Preferred Alternatives

| Instead of | Say |
|-----------|-----|
| "We utilize eco-friendly disposal methods" | "We recycle and donate what we can — the rest goes to licensed facilities" |
| "Our comprehensive junk removal services" | "We haul everything from couches to construction debris" |
| "Navigate the complexities of junk disposal" | "Figure out what goes where" |
| "Leverage our expertise" | "We know the area" |
| "We are committed to customer satisfaction" | "We show up on time and clean up after ourselves" |

---

## Writing Rhythm

- **Short sentences.** Then a longer one that adds detail or context. Then short again.
- Paragraphs: 2-4 sentences max. If it's getting long, break it up.
- Use specific numbers: "$249 quarter-load" beats "affordable pricing"
- Name real places: "off the 8 freeway near Grossmont" beats "conveniently located"
- Use "we" and "our" naturally — this is a real team talking

---

## Local Knowledge Signals

Good content sounds like it was written by someone who lives here. Include:

- Actual neighborhood names (not just city names)
- Real street names and landmarks
- Zip codes
- Local geography references (hills, canyons, coastal bluffs)
- Weather specifics (June Gloom, Santa Ana winds, summer heat in east county)
- Known local challenges (narrow streets in Hillcrest, hillside access in La Jolla, HOA rules in Carmel Valley)
- Distance/time from La Mesa HQ: "about 20 minutes from our base in La Mesa"

---

## Service Scope — Hard Boundary

Severin Hauling does **junk removal and hauling**. That's it.

**Always OK to mention**: furniture removal, appliance removal, mattress disposal, hot tub removal, piano removal, estate cleanouts, hoarding cleanup, construction debris, concrete disposal, commercial junk removal, foreclosure/eviction cleanouts, scrap metal removal, emergency and same-day service.

**Never mention**: cleaning, moving, organizing, Airbnb turnovers, equestrian anything, property management, landscaping, demolition (beyond debris removal), storage.

If you're not sure whether a service is offered, don't mention it.

## CTA Voice

CTAs should be direct and specific:

| Good | Bad |
|------|-----|
| "Call (619) 750-0114 for a free estimate" | "Contact us today to learn more" |
| "Single items start at $69 — call now" | "Get in touch for affordable pricing" |
| "We're available 24/7 — call or text" | "Reach out to our team of professionals" |
| "Book your pickup today" | "Take the first step toward a clutter-free home" |
